History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small meetup for tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into one of the largest technology conferences in the world, attracting innovators, developers, and industry leaders. Our mission has always been to inspire and connect the global tech community.

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to foster innovation and collaboration in technology. We aim to provide a platform where visionaries share ideas, developers collaborate, and businesses innovate together to shape the future of technology.

Past Speakers

Dr. Ada Lovelace

Dr. Ada Lovelace, a pioneer in computer science, inspired many in the field of technology with her visionary ideas. She spoke at TechCon 2018 about the future of computational creativity.

Mr. Alan Turing

Mr. Alan Turing, celebrated for his work in AI and cryptography, delivered a keynote in TechCon 2019 about the ethical implications of artificial intelligence.
